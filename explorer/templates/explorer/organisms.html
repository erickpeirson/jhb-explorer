Organisms in the JHB

The living organism is the premise and backbone of the biological sciences, and is thus an important dimension of the thematic content of JHB. Using a technique called Named Entity Recognition, and drawing on the NCBI Taxonomy database, we have identified the taxa discussed by historians of science over the past fifty years. Use the taxon browser below to explore the role of organisms in scholarship about the history of biology.
