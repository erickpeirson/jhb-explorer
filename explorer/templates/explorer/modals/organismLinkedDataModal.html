Linked Data

In the broadest sense, the term linked data refers to practices and technologies that make it easier to find related resources on the internet. The NCBI LinkOut service provides information about online resources external to NCBI that are related to specific NCBI database entries (in this case, taxa in the NCBI Taxonomy database).

The Linked Data panel in the lower right section of the page shows the resources that we retrieved for {{ taxon|format_taxon_name|safe }}, ranging from gene sequence data to phylogenetic trees.

Methodological Notes

Named Entity Recognition performed with LINNAEUS. Taxonomic information and linked data retrieved from the NCBI Taxonomy database. Visualization developed in D3.js.
