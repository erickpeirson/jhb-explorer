The topic model at the heart of this online exhibit describes how latent themes—observed indirectly as clusters of co-occurring words—are distributed across thousands of pages of articles in the Journal of the History of Biology. The excitement for LDA topic modeling in digital humanities circles has obscured the fact that these models were designed primarily for optimizing online search engines, and not for scholarly research! In the Related Articles panel, we use the distribution of latent topics as a way to cluster together articles that contain similar themes.
